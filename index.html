Project title

Logo animation

Description

The motion builds around the “nerdy” elements of the logo — subtle character details, playful references, and sharp, confident typography. I kept the animation tight and intentional, letting the icons reveal themselves without overdoing the effects. The goal was to reflect the studio’s personality: bold, slightly geeky, and self-aware — while keeping the final result clean enough to work across reels, promos and digital intros.

Software: After Effects, Illustrator

Project title

Email interaction animation

Description

A playful motion piece built around everyday digital communication. Icons orbit and interact around the main message element, creating a light, friendly rhythm. The animation focuses on layering, soft depth, and smooth easing to keep the composition dynamic without feeling chaotic. Designed to feel approachable and clear — suitable for explainer videos, UI promos, or social content related to messaging and interaction.

Software: After Effects, Illustrator

Project title

Motion tracking

Description

Text pinned in 3D space on drone footage for a simple “project in progress” callout. Short reel created for INSITE EPM where typography is tracked directly onto drone footage of an active construction site. The phrase “Project in progress” was pinned in 3D space to sit naturally on the building surface, following camera movement and perspective shifts. The goal was to keep it subtle and integrated — not flashy — so the message feels part of the environment rather than placed on top of it.

Software: After Effects
